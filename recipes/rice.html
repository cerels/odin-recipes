Rice

Say goodbye to scorched pans and gummy grains, and learn how to cook rice like a pro! These easy stovetop methods yield tender, fluffy rice every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice, well rinsed
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine the rinsed rice, water, and olive oil in a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Cover, reduce the heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and let it sit, covered for 10 more minutes.
  4. Fluff with a fork.
